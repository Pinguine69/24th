We are updating this page. For information on part-time tuition or Saturday classes, please contact the college directly.

Who We Are

We provide small group classes and one-to-one private tuition. With a maximum group size of five, children study in a quiet classroom environment and teachers can pay individual attention.

What We Do

We deliver expert 11+ tuition and entrance exam preparation using proven frameworks and resources. Our 10+/11+ offering is tailored to the schools of your choice in your local area and supports scholarship ambition.

How We Approach

Our course covers all four 10+/11+ subjects. We build strong foundations in core maths and English and use technology to personalise materials. Tutors provide individual attention and regular progress tests to identify and resolve weaknesses.

GCSE Saturday Classes

GCSE classes for English, Maths and Science run every Saturday from 9:00-13:00. These classes aim to:

  • Strengthen subject knowledge
  • Improve exam techniques
  • Increase confidence
  • Resolve any problematic areas

To book, call 020 8688 5777 or email Reception@croydonmet.org.uk.

Extra Tuition

We offer extra tuition for ages 10+, Years 7-9, GCSE, AS and A2 students. Classes are held during weekday evenings and on Saturdays. Tuition can cover any subject areas you need support with. Verbal and non-verbal reasoning practice is also tutored. You may book one-to-one tuition, a pair (two pupils) or larger small groups. We also run revision classes during half term and the Easter holidays.

We run 11+, KS3, GCSE, AS and A2 classes every Saturday. If a subject you want is not currently running, please call 020 8688 5777 and we will do what we can to assist.

If you would like to join any of our running classes, please complete our part-time application and email it to Reception@croydonmet.org.uk, or use our Contact & Apply page.
