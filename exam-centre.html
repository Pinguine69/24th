Exam Centre

CMC accepts private candidates for GCSE and A-Level across major boards (AQA, Pearson Edexcel, OCR). Our Exams Officer provides clear guidance on entries, deadlines and ID requirements.

GCSE Exam Entries

We offer flexible entry windows for core and elective GCSE subjects. Documentation required includes proof of ID, previous candidate details and payment of the entry fee.

  • Access to practice papers and invigilator briefings.
  • Option to book supervised mocks to build exam confidence.
  • Results collected on-site or forwarded securely upon release.

A-Level Exam Entries

We register A-Level and AS candidates for summer and autumn sessions. Speak with the exams office if you require science practical endorsements or coursework components.

  • Guidance on subject codes, entry deadlines and resit policies.
  • Support with predicted grades and UCAS references where appropriate.
  • Disability access arrangements arranged in line with JCQ regulations.

Exam Policies & T&C

  • ID must match the legal name on exam entries.
  • Centre regulations follow JCQ guidelines; malpractice will void entries.
  • Fees are non-refundable once entries are confirmed with the awarding body.

Exam Registration

  1. Submit the interest form (see Contact & Apply).
  2. We confirm subject availability, deadlines and fees.
  3. Make payment and receive your exam timetable and candidate information.

Exam Centre FAQs

Which exam boards do you work with?

We register candidates across AQA, Pearson Edexcel and OCR. Contact us if you require other boards so we can confirm availability.

Can I sit science practicals with you?

Yes, practical endorsements are available for most science A-Levels. Spaces are limited so please enquire early.

When will I receive my results?

Results are released on the published JCQ dates. You can collect them in person or we can email them securely to your chosen address.
