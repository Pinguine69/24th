Who We Are

Croydon Metropolitan College is equipped to provide small group classes and one-to-one private tuition. With a maximum class size of five, children study in a calm classroom environment and teachers can offer individual attention.

What We Do

We provide expert 11+ tuition and entrance exam preparation. Our framework, tools and methodologies have helped many students secure places at top grammar and independent schools, often with scholarship offers.

How We Approach

Our course covers all four 10+/11+ subjects. We build strong foundations in core maths and English, personalise materials with technology, and run regular progress tests to identify and resolve weaknesses.
