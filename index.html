Welcome to

Croydon Metropolitan College

Croydon Metropolitan College is a co-educational day college specialising in GCSE, A-Level and tailored retake programmes. Our small classes and personalised pathways support ambitious learners from London and beyond.

Founded in the heart of Croydon, we combine rigorous academics with dedicated pastoral care so every student is known, challenged and encouraged. Whether you're progressing to top universities, preparing for entrance exams or seeking flexible part-time study, our experienced tutors and exam-centre expertise help you move forward with confidence.

International Links with Zambia

CMC has twinned with Headteacher Mr George Mulubwa at Chikungu school in Chipata, Zambia. This gives opportunities for students in both colleges to make friends with students from different cultural backgrounds.

This exciting development will ultimately lead to student exchanges. Currently CMC is helping to raise money for text books for the Zambian school. Students at Chikungu speak both English and Chichewe, Zikomo kwambiri!
